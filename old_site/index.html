Welcome to Glen Shelly Auto Brokers. We are a brokerage and sales company specializing in high quality, hard to find cars and motorcycles. We deal in practical and efficient sedans and SUVs as well as high performance sports cars. The one thing that all of our cars have in common is their exceptional condition both cosmetically and mechanically. We are always on the lookout for particular makes and model years of cars and motorcycles that represent the best vehicles on the pre-owned market.

 

At our office in Erie, CO, we keep a small inventory of vehicles that we have acquired through wholesale and retail sources throughout the country. We also love to search for the exact vehicle that meets our customer’s need, so please contact us to discuss what you’re looking for.
