It is the perfect balance between technological advancement, driving experience, luxury, and reliability. The 2005-model year is the best year and came equipped with the most standard options. The M54B30 3.0 liter inline six cylinder engine found in the 330xi produces 230 horsepower and 211 foot/pounds of torque (available at 3500 rpm) delivered in perfect linear fashion. The dual VANOS unit offers infinitely variable timing to optimize both power and efficiency. It is also extremely reliable. I have seen examples of this engine go well beyond 300,000 miles if properly maintained. Of course, if you are shopping for a BMW, you already know this.

E46's produced after the latter half of the 2003 model year were equipped with a more reliable 5 speed automatic transmission produced by GM (ironically). I have always been a manual fanatic, but added an E46 with an automatic gearbox to my personal fleet a few years ago. I have to confess that it makes negotiating Denver's traffic downright civilized. It has a true manual mode, where it will hold a gear against the rev-limiter as long as you ask it to; a fantastic feature for climbing Colorado's steep mountain passes. It will also automatically downshift under moderate braking, a fantastic feature for control and efficiency on the way down!

I've always believed a car with good driving dynamics equipped with the right snow tires is more important in the snow and ice than just AWD. Adding AWD to the 50/50-balance of the BMW E46 3 series only increases driver confidence. The E46 all wheel drive system is mechanically simple, resulting in long lasting reliability. It uses a planetary gear-based system, not a haldex or torsen-based system found in VW and Audi or Subaru. It is simple, but highly effective, especially when combined with Dynamic Stability Control (which can be turned off). The power is split 38% front / 68% rear, preserving the rear wheel drive dynamics that define the BMW driving experience without compromising traction in adverse conditions. When the weather turns ugly, you will take on the snow with the confidence found only in the Ultimate Driving Machine.
