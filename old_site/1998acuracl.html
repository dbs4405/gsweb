This is a beautiful 1998 Acura 3.0 CL with only 54,000 miles in excellent condition. It is a 2 owner vehicle with a clean Carfax showing no accidents. 
 

The exterior:

The overall exterior condition is very good.

The paint appears glossy all over. There are several door dings but no major dents or scratches. The glass on all six windows is in excellent condition with no chips or cracks.

 

The interior:

The overall condition of the interior is excellent.

The leather is in excellent condition with no holes, stains or excessive wear. The carpet also shows very little wear and OEM floor mats are included. The vinyl dash and door panels, as well as all interior trim is in excellent condition.

 

Mechanically:

All systems function as they should and nothing is broken or inoperative on this vehicle. The engine runs and the vehicle handles with no unusual sounds or vibrations. The transmission shifts as it should with no abnormal sounds or difficulty. The electrical system works as is should as well as all electrical components. The window motors and regulators all operate at normal speed with no unusual sounds.
