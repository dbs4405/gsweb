2005 Volkswagon Passat GLX Wagon V6 4Motion - SOLD
41,500 miles

This is a beautiful 2005 Volkswagen Passat GLX station wagon with only 41,500 miles in excellent condition. It was purchased in 2005 from Emich VW in Denver and has been driven locally ever since. I bought this car from its original owner and have driven it approximately 3000 miles in the last 5 months. We have all service records for this vehicle and a clean carfax showing no accidents and one original owner other than myself.  The all-season Goodyear Eagle GT tires were purchased just 2 months ago and have less than 500 miles on them. The alternator was replaced in June of this year.

 

The exterior:

The overall exterior condition is excellent.

The paint appears glossy all over and has no major dents or scratches. The glass on all six windows is in excellent condition with no chips or cracks.

 

The interior:

The overall condition of the interior is excellent.

The leather is in excellent condition with no holes, stains or excessive wear. The carpet also shows very little wear and OEM rubber floor mats are included. Also included are the original carpet floor mats that have never been used. The vinyl dash and door panels, as well as all interior trim is in excellent condition.

 

Mechanically:

All systems function as they should and nothing is broken or inoperative on this vehicle. The engine runs and the vehicle handles with no unusual sounds or vibrations. The transmission shifts as it should with no abnormal sounds or difficulty. The electrical system works as is should as well as all electrical components. The window motors and regulators all operate at normal speed with no unusual sounds.
